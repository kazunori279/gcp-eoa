## Improvement plan for `m4.html`

- **P1 — Make `gemini-2.5-flash` the default judge.** The prompt specifies
  `gemini-2.5-flash-lite` (with a fallback note). In this lab flash-lite wasn't used; agy ran
  the judge on `gemini-2.5-flash`. Since flash-lite is often not enabled in lab projects,
  consider defaulting to `gemini-2.5-flash` (and noting flash-lite as the faster option) to
  avoid an extra fix step.
- **P2 — Mention the required `eval_set_id` field.** agy's first `adk eval` failed because the
  evalset lacked a top-level `eval_set_id`; it then added it. A one-line note in the prompt
  ("the evalset needs a top-level `eval_set_id`") would save that iteration.
- **P3 — (Tried & reverted) the simulation step is monitor-heavy.** agy spent several cycles
  polling its own background sim task. We tried adding a "run in the foreground" instruction,
  but the re-run proved it **ineffective** — agy's `run_command` auto-backgrounds long-running
  scripts regardless of the prompt. The wording was **reverted**; this poll-wait is inherent to
  agy and isn't fixable via the prompt. (Not a content bug.)
